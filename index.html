Scarlet Room
Grill & Bar

Welcome to Scarlet Room, a vibrant and elegant restaurant located in the heart of Amsterdam. Our restaurant offers a unique dining experience that combines modern cuisine with a touch of sophistication. At Scarlet Room, we believe that food should be more than just sustenance. It should be an experience that tantalizes your senses and leaves you with memories to cherish. Our menu features a variety of dishes that are expertly prepared using only the freshest and highest quality ingredients.

Our Promise

Our chefs draw inspiration from both traditional and contemporary culinary techniques to create dishes that are not only delicious but also visually stunning. Whether you're in the mood for a hearty steak or a light seafood dish, our menu has something to satisfy every palate. In addition to our exceptional cuisine, Scarlet Room boasts an extensive wine list that features a variety of local and international wines. Our expert sommeliers are always on hand to help you choose the perfect wine to complement your meal.

The Scarlet Room restaurant was founded by a woman named Scarlet van der Meer. Scarlet had always been passionate about cooking and had dreamed of opening her own restaurant for years. However, she struggled to find the perfect location for her establishment. One day, while exploring the city of Amsterdam, Scarlet stumbled upon an old abandoned building that caught her eye. The building was in disrepair and had been left untouched for years, but Scarlet could see the potential in its high ceilings and large windows.

Review

I must say that the Scarlet Room is a true hidden gem. Walking into the restaurant, you'll immediately be struck by the dim lighting and the sensual decor.
But the real star of the show is the food. The menu is small, but every dish is executed to perfection. The chefs clearly put a lot of thought and care into every plate. I highly recommend starting with the scallop ceviche, which is both light and flavorful. The steak with truffle sauce is another standout dish that is sure to leave you wanting more.
